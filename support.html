Support

for CrowdNotes

CrowdNotes is designed to help teams collaborate during recruiting or networking events by sharing notes about attendees using a shared Google Spreadsheet.

Getting Help

If you experience issues using the CrowdNotes app, have questions about features, or want to report a bug, please contact the developer.

Developer: Joe Ghalam
Email: jghalam@yahoo.com

Common Issues

Most issues are related to access permissions for the Google Spreadsheet used by the app. Please ensure that:

  • The spreadsheet link entered in the app is correct.
  • The spreadsheet has been shared with the appropriate users.
  • Internet connectivity is available.
