About GhalamRadio.

GhalamRadio is an iOS app that connects you to thousands of online radio stations worldwide. Search by name, call letters, frequency, or geographic proximity — then save your favorites for instant access.

The app does not collect or store any personal information. No accounts are required. Your saved stations are stored locally on your device.
