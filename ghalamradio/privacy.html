Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

Joe Ghalam built GhalamRadio as a free app. This service is provided by Joe Ghalam and is intended for use as-is. This page informs you of the policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use GhalamRadio.

By using GhalamRadio, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information Collection and Use

GhalamRadio requires access to the following in order to function:

  • Location services (optional) — used only when you search for nearby radio stations by geographic radius. Your location is used in real-time for this search and is not stored or transmitted to any server controlled by this app.
  • Network access — required to search for stations and stream audio from online radio sources.

No personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or transmitted by GhalamRadio. Your saved station list is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

GhalamRadio stores only your saved station list and app preferences (such as display settings) locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

GhalamRadio retrieves radio station data from a third-party radio directory service to power search results. This interaction involves network requests but does not transmit any personal information about you. No advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, or tracking libraries are used in the app.

Log Data

In the event of an app error, iOS may generate crash diagnostic data including device model, operating system version, app version, and time of the error. This is handled through Apple's standard platform crash reporting and is subject to Apple's privacy practices.

Cookies

GhalamRadio does not use cookies or any equivalent tracking technology.

Children's Privacy

GhalamRadio does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. No registration, accounts, or data submission features exist in the app.

Links to Other Sites

GhalamRadio does not contain links to external websites within the app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after updates are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
