About GhalamPhotos.

GhalamPhotos is an iOS app that displays photos from your selected Apple Photos album as a continuous, beautiful slideshow on your device — turning your iPhone or iPad into a dedicated digital photo frame.

The app accesses your photos solely for display purposes and makes no changes to your library. No photos are stored outside your device, and no personal data is transmitted or retained beyond your preferences.

Features.

🖼️

Apple Photos

Browse and select any album from your Apple Photos library.

🎞️

Smooth Transitions

Choose from dissolve, slide, and other animated transitions between photos.

🎬

Video Playback

Videos in your album play automatically, muted, then advance to the next item.

🗺️

Location & Map

Displays the photo's location with a live map thumbnail when available.

🌤️

Live Weather

Shows current local weather and temperature on the always-visible bottom bar.

🎉

Holiday Animations

Festive GIF overlays and particle effects for holidays and birthdays.

📅

Birthday Reminders

Reads your calendar to display birthday announcements on the screen.

⚙️

Customizable

Adjust slideshow interval, transition style, display toggles, and more.
