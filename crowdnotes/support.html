Common Questions

Do I need an iCloud account?
Yes. CrowdNotes uses Apple CloudKit to store and sync data. You'll need to be signed into iCloud on your device.

How do I share an event with my team?
Open the event, tap the share button, and invite collaborators using their Apple ID. They'll receive an iCloud sharing invitation.

Is my data backed up?
All data is stored in iCloud and is subject to iCloud's backup and sync policies. Ensure iCloud is enabled on your device.
