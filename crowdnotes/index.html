About CrowdNotes.

CrowdNotes is a lightweight iOS collaboration app designed for recruiting fairs, networking events, and any gathering where a team needs to track and share notes about attendees in real time.

Team members can search for attendees by name or scan a QR code assigned during registration, then add notes that sync instantly to the whole team via Apple iCloud. No separate servers, no third-party accounts — just your Apple ID.

Features.

🔍

Quick Lookup

Search attendees by name instantly during a live event.

📷

QR Code Scan

Scan a QR code on an attendee's badge to pull up their record immediately.

📝

Shared Notes

Add notes about attendees that sync in real time to the entire team.

☁️

iCloud Powered

All data lives in your iCloud account. No sign-up or external servers required.

👥

Team Collaboration

Invite team members to share an event and see each other's notes live.
