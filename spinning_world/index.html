A geography game inspired by the classic spinning globe — pick a random spot on Earth and race to identify the country before the clock runs out.

About Spinning World.

Before smartphones, geography buffs would spin a physical globe, stop it with a finger, and challenge themselves to identify wherever it landed. Spinning World brings that same spirit to iOS, powered by Apple Maps.

Tap any land location on the map to drop a pin, then race to name the country from a list before your 30-second window closes. Two difficulty modes let you play by feel or with visible borders — perfect for all skill levels.

How to Play.

🌍

Pick a Location

Tap anywhere on the map to drop a pin on a land mass. Water and invalid taps are rejected.

🔒

Hard Mode

Country names and borders are hidden. Identify the country by continent and general geography alone.

🗺️

Easy Mode

Country borders are shown on the map. The closer you zoom in, the more detail you can see.

⏳

30-Second Timer

Once you're ready to guess, the clock starts. Select the correct country from the list to score.

👁️

Reveal Hint

Tap the counter during the countdown to reveal the location — but it costs you 1 point.

🔄

Change Your Pick

Not happy with your pin? Tap a new location or press Delete to start fresh.

Scoring.

Points are awarded based on how quickly you identify the correct country. Every wrong guess or moment of hesitation counts against you.
