Privacy Policy

for CrowdNotes

Last updated: March 2026

CrowdNotes ("the App") is designed to help teams collaborate during recruiting or networking events by sharing notes about attendees. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses and how that information is used.

Information Accessed by the App

CrowdNotes accesses data stored in a Google Spreadsheet provided by the user. The spreadsheet link is entered by the user and must already have appropriate sharing permissions configured.

The spreadsheet may contain information such as:

  • Attendee names
  • Basic contact information
  • Event-related notes entered by team members
  • QR code identifiers assigned to attendees

How the Information Is Used

The App reads and writes information to the provided Google Spreadsheet in order to:

  • Pull up attendee information in the app during an event
  • Allow event team members to add notes about attendees
  • Allow lookup of attendees by name or QR code
  • Enable collaboration and note sharing between event staff using the same spreadsheet as a central sharing place

Google Services

CrowdNotes uses Google APIs to access Google Sheets data when the user provides a spreadsheet URL. The App only accesses the specific spreadsheet supplied by the user and does not access other files in the user's Google account.

Data Storage

CrowdNotes does not operate its own servers and does not store attendee information outside of the Google Spreadsheet provided by the user.

All data remains in the spreadsheet managed by the spreadsheet owner.

Permissions and Security

CrowdNotes does not create, change, or manage Google Spreadsheet sharing permissions. The spreadsheet owner is responsible for configuring access permissions and managing the security of the data contained in the spreadsheet.

Data Sharing

CrowdNotes does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. All collaboration occurs directly through the shared spreadsheet provided by the user.

Children's Privacy

CrowdNotes is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
