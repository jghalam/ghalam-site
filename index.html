CrowdNotes

Collaborative event recruiting notes

CrowdNotes is a lightweight collaboration app designed for recruiting or networking events. Event staff can quickly look up attendees and share notes about conversations during the event.

The app connects to a Google Spreadsheet provided by the event organizer. Team members can search for attendees or scan a QR code assigned during registration and add notes in real time.

CrowdNotes is provided for free and contains no in-app purchases.
