CrowdNotes

CrowdNotes is a lightweight collaboration app designed for small recruiting or networking events. It allows event team members to view attendees and share notes about conversations during the event.

The app connects to a Google Spreadsheet provided by the event organizer. Team members can search for attendees or scan their assigned QR code and add notes during the event.

CrowdNotes is a free app with no in-app purchases.
